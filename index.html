You might know that getting an ISI Internship is not an easy task. We had some constraints too. We can only allow a handful of students for summer Internship. While other applicant's will get a chance to work at ISI Calcutta on various challenging open source projects under faculty supervision & guidance.

Some Details:

What is meant by Internship: This means you get a chance to attend theoretical classes on Cryptology by various faculty members at ISI. After that you will be assigned some projects.
What is meant by Project: While in this case, there will be no formal classes but your guide / faculty will help you understand the project problem and you will have to code the solution.
